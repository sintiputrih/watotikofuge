Singer Kelly Rowland announces birth of second child WSOC TV

Destiny’s Child has a new member.

>> Read more trending news

Singer Kelly Rowland, who performed in the trio during the late 1990s, announced the birth of her second child on Saturday, E! Online reported.

Rowland, 39, and Tim Weatherspoon had their second son, Noah Jon Weatherspoon, on Jan. 21.

“On the 21st day, of the 21st year, of the 21st century, Noah Jon Weatherspoon greeted us! We are truly grateful,” Rowland wrote on Instagram, alongside a photo of her and Tim’s eldest son, 6-year-old Titan Jewell Weatherspoon, who is bonding with his baby brother.

Noah Weatherspoon was born at 8:13 p.m. and weighed 7 pounds, 8 ounces, the post said. He was also 19 inches long at birth.

Rowland revealed her pregnancy in October with a cover story that appeared in Women’s Health magazine.

“We had been talking about it loosely, and then COVID happened, and we were just like, ‘Let’s see what happens,’” the Atlanta native told the magazine. “Being able to have a child … I’m knocking at 40′s door in February. Taking care of myself means a lot to me.”

Rowland said at the time that she was concerned that her fans would be upset about her having a baby before releasing her fifth studio album, E! Online reported.

“I was thinking, ‘Oh my God, my fans are gonna be so disappointed. They wanted an album first, but they got a baby!’” Rowland said. “And I was like, ‘I have to figure this out so they get both.’”
