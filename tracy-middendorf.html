Biography

Tracy Middendorf was born in Miami, FL on January 26, 1970. TV, movie, and stage actress known for her roles as Maggie Duval on Scream: The TV Series, Babette on Boardwalk Empire, and Laura Kingman on Beverly Hills 90210. She also appeared in the movies El Cortez and Reaching for the Moon. She starred alongside Ele Keats in Snowflake. After dropping out of high school in 1987, she studied acting and attended SUNY Purchase. She landed her first TV role in 1992 as Carrie Brady on Days of Our Lives.
