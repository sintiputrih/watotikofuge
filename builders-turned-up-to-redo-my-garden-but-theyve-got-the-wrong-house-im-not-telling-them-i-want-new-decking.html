WE ALL dream of having a luxury garden, but the price often turns us off entirely.

So when builders mistakenly rocked up to one woman’s home and started renovating hers she wasn’t going to stop them, or at least that’s what she led on.

Homeowner Chloe Fountainn was at home when the workmen reportedly showed up unannounced and began dumping huge loads of rocks in her garden, but she claims it was the wrong house.

Chloe filmed as the incident unfold and shared a series of clips on her TikTok which supposedly saw the builders renovate her garden or free – although many think it’s a joke.

Rather than running outside to stop them in their tracks, Chloe claimed she was going to let it ride out so she could score a new decking.

There was one problem though, Chloe doesn’t actually know what work is being done, or least that’s what she claims in the videos

Whether it’s a brand new decking or a sleek concrete slab she can’t be sure, but she doesn’t mind so long as she gets a new garden.

“You’ve got the wrong house! You have the wrong house! Oh no, you have the wrong house,” Chloe can be heard saying from behind her closed door, in her first clip.

“This is a huge mess up,” she continues but not loud enough for them to hear.

In a follow up clip, Chloe added: “Well, they’re at the wrong house but I’m not going to stop them because I’d love to have free concrete – or a deck, whatever they’re building. I’ll let you know in part three how it looks.”

The builders continue to dig up her existing garden and Chloe revealed in a follow up clip that she had no intention of telling them until it was all done – although we can’t be sure if this is true.

“Okay, so they’re here now, but obviously they’re not finished and there’s no way I’m telling them about it,” she said.

“I’m going to tell them when they’re done. I want whatever they’re doing down, and then I’m going to be, like, ‘Wrong house!'”

Updating her followers on the progress so far, the homeowner claimed she still had no idea what they were building in her garden saying it was still going to be a surprise to her.

“It’s a complete surprise because they’re at the wrong house, right?” she said. “So if you know what this is going to be – it’s all stone – let me know.”

While it’s not known how serious Chloe is or if her claims are true, many found the incident hilarious.

However some didn’t believe Chloe and said refusing to pay would be considered theft – but she reassured everyone she would end up paying.

“Sorry to tell you that what you’re doing is illegal because you could’ve stopped them before it happened,” one user commented.

Meanwhile, another added: “The fact that y’all believe this is real is hilarious…”
