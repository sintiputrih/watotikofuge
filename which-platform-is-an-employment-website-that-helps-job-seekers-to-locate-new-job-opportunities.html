Which platform is employment website that helps jobseekers to locate new job opportunities?

What are job search keywords?

First, a quick definition: A keyword, when used to job search, is a word or term that is related to the type of employment you are searching for. When you search for a job by keyword, all the positions that contain the word or term you entered will be listed in the posting.

    What is Applicant Tracking System in HR?

    An applicant tracking system (ATS) is software that manages the recruiting and hiring process, including job postings and job applications. It organizes information about job seekers and makes it searchable. As its name implies, an ATS tracks candidates through the hiring process.

    Can ATS read bullets?
