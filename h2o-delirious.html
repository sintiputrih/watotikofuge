H2O Delirious: Wiki, Height, Age, Face, Net worth, Biography &amp; More

H2O Delirious (born 2 May 1987) is a famous YouTuber & video game commentator from North Carolina, USA. He is best known for his fun gaming content, famously associated with the Vanoss gaming team.

Started in 2007, this YouTuber has a baffling 13.1 million subscribers[1]YouTube on his channel. His content is mainly video game observation content where he reviews and makes commentary on popular games like ‘Grand Auto Theft’, ‘Call of Duty’, ‘FallOut’, ‘Black Ops II’, and many more.

H2O Delirious is yet to reveal his real face.

His unique way of commenting and reviewing is loved by fans all over the world. The added touch of his quirky laughter makes for bonus points.

YouTube Career & Net Worth

In 2007, a young American boy launched his YouTube channel & was quick to gain attention for wearing his infamous ‘Jason’ masks as seen in the films of ‘Friday the 13th’.

His original name tag when he first started was “EXP I Delirious”. Later he renamed it to his current nickname. But in 2010, he changed this name again to “H2O Delicious” only to change it back in the same year.

The following year, he liked one of Vanoss’s videos, who is an Internet personality and gamer. Once when Vanoss was playing “Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III”, Delirious messaged him for a request to play together. This marked the beginning of a new friendship and collaborative alliance.

H2O Delirious pranked his fans twice with fake face reveals.

Single-player gaming videos are the most popular videos on his channel. Along with famous games like “Call of Duty”, he also plays horror indie games like “Outlast”, “The Evil Within”, “Five Nights at Freddy’s  & 4”, etc.

He pulled two fake face review pranks on his followers. In 2015, he showed his GTAV character face instead of his real face and pranked his fans. He did a similar thing in 2017 with the help of fellow YouTubers, Daithi de Nogla and Miniladd.
