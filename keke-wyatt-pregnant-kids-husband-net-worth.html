Keke Wyatt Married Life & Kids

Keke Wyatt is a renowned singer and songwriter and she is also a mother to 10 children and going to be mother of her 11th child. From her first ever marriage with her road manager, Rahmat Morton and she gave birth to three children named Keyver Wyatt, Rahjah Ke' and Ke'Tarah Victoria.

They welcomed four children to their family and they are named Ke'Mar Von, Wyatt Michael, Ke'Yoshi Bella and Kendall Miguel. The pair got divorced in August 2018. Keke even posted on her social media about how Michael called her an emotional wreck when she was 8 months pregnant. After that, Keke moved on with her ex-boyfriend, Zackariah David Darring and got married with him in 2018 and lived together. They share Ke'Riah David and Zazkariah together. Their third child together and altogether 11th child is on the way. Keke's present husband, Zackariah Darring is a businessman.

Net Worth Of Keke Wyatt

Keke Wyatt has a net worth of approximately $4 million as an American R&B singer, songwriter, actress and reality star. She was born on March 10, 1982 and comes from Indianapolis, Indiana. She started performing at the age of five and performed with several girl bands in her teenage years. 

She got her main start when she collaborated with R&B singer Avant and then she released five studio albums like Soul Sister, Who Knew?, Unbelieveable, Rated Love and Keke Covers. She also has a EP named after herself, Ke'Ke'.

She starred and joined the cast of R&B Divas: Atlanta in 2017 and appeared on the show for three seasons. She is also known for her work with several non-profit organizations. She was a spokesperson for the National Coalition Against Domestic Violence.

Keke Wyatt has given her musical talent credit to her parents and shared that her mother, Lorna Wyatt was a vocalist and her faher, Keever Wyatt II was an organist in church. She said that her parents encouraged her to pursue a caree in non-religious music. She also talked about her parents in an interview and complimented her mother's voice to be sounded like Tramaine Hawkins. She also said that she inherited her vocal chops from her mother.

Some of her best songs are Used to Love, Nothing in This World, Tap Out, Unbelieveable, If Only You Knew, So Confused, On Repeat, Love Reigns, Love Me and so on.
