See Kylie Jenner Channel Quiet Luxury in Chic White Ensembles

Kylie Jenner is entering a new era for her street style.

The beauty mogul has spent the past weekend out and about in the French capital, where she's worn some very elevated looks. While the Kylie Cosmetics founder is best known for skintight silhouettes, trendy fabrics, and dramatic detailing, this weekend has been all about quiet luxury ensembles that scream Parisian chic (while also reminding onlookers of her old friend Sofia Richie).

For an evening outing last night, Jenner looked very glamorous in a white Alaïa halter dress, which featured a high neckline, a corseted drop waist, and a flowing ankle-length skirt. She finished the monochromatic outfit with strappy heeled sandals and a small top-handle purse. For her effortless glam look, the star wore her hair tied back with a white hair tie, leaving several tendrils framing her subtle smoky eye, rosy cheeks, and nude matte lip.

MEGA//Getty Images

Earlier that day, the Kardashians star wore another elegant white ensemble during a luxury shopping trip that included a trip to Chanel's Paris boutique. Her two-piece set came courtesy of Maximilian Davis's fall/winter 2022 collection at Fashion East, composed of a pleated A-line miniskirt and a cropped blazer featuring a mock-neck and asymmetrical front.

She completed the look with simple black heels and thick silver hoops, while carrying a small handbag and wearing thin oval sunglasses. Her raven hair was worn loose and paired with a nude pink lip.

MEGA//Getty Images

On Saturday, Jenner stepped out for lunch in a 1950s-evoking black halter dress by Bottega Veneta. The midi-length piece included a plunging V-shaped neckline, a bustier bodice, and a flowing skirt with a slight asymmetrical hem. She completed the luxe look with black pointed-toe heels, while her accessories included glamorous black sunglasses, a black purse also by Bottega, and chunky gold earrings.
