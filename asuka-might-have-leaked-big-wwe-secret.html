Asuka Might Have Leaked Big WWE Secret

You have to be really careful what you put out there on social media. It seems that Asuka might have jumped the gun on a tweet and spilled some big news. WWE isn’t set to reveal SummerSlam 2021’s location until the Belmont Preshow tomorrow on NBC.

WWE is considering several NFL stadiums for SummerSlam. They originally wanted Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas, and that is why the date was switched to Saturday.

Asuka tweeted out a single photo along with a “shocked” emoji. The photo was of Allegiant Stadium. The company will run an NFL football stadium for SummerSlam. Those locations include: Hard Rock Stadium in Miami, SoFi Stadium in Inglewood, Calif., NRG Stadium in Houston, Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas, MetLife Stadium, and Nissan Stadium in Nashville.

It appears that Allegiant Stadium is going to be SummerSlam’s home after all. This is interesting because they won’t be the hottest ticket in town. Manny Pacquiao’s return to boxing is also taking place in Las Vegas on the same night.
