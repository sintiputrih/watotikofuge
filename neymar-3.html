Neymar also known as Neymar da Silva Santos Junior, is a Brazilian footballer. As of 2022, Neymar’s net worth is $200 million. He plays as a forward for Paris Saint Germain and the Brazil National Team. He is widely regarded as one of the best players in the world.

Neymar first gained notoriety with Santos, when he made his professional debut at the age of 17. He assisted the team in winning two consecutive Campeonato Paulista titles, a Copa do Brasil, and the 2011 Copa Libertadores; the latter was Santos’ first Copa Libertadores title since 1963.
