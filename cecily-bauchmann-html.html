Cecily Bauchmann - Trivia, Family, Bio

Vlogger who focuses on mothering and family life. Her topics range from beauty, fashion, routines, cooking, parenting, and home organization. She has gone viral for her comedy sketches and her get-ready-with-me vlogs. She has amassed 2 million fans. 

Before Fame

She became active on TikTok in June 2019. 

Trivia

Her cecilybauchmann Instagram account displays her Christian faith, her love of style, and a variety of cooking tutorials. One of her most popular Instagram reels features her Sunday morning routine with her young children. The realistic video has garnered over 2 million views.

Family Life

She was born in the United States. She and her husband have two boys and two girls as well as a pet poodle together. Her sister-in-law is Instagram influencer Samantha Bauchmann.  

Associated With

In January of 2023, she posted a TikTok set to "Monkeys Spinning Monkeys" by Kevin MacLeod.
