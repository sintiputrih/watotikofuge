Big Spoiler On CM Punks Wrestling Gear At AEW Rampage Grand Slam

CM Punk surprised fans when he wrestled in full tights at All Out. That match against Darby Allin made some fans think he decided to go with that look for his next run in pro wrestling, but that was not the case in Arthur Ashe Stadium. This article contains spoilers, so please keep that in mind.

Click here for our complete spoilers for AEW Rampage Grand Slam

CM Punk came out to wrestle Will Hobbs at AEW Rampage Grand Slam and he was sporting his old style of trunks. Many fans missed that look and it appears that Punk might have listened to them.

Punk keeps all of his own pro wrestling gear. He made point to say how creepy he finds it that fans buy pro wrestling gear and he’s not selling it. We’ll have to see if CM Punk goes back to the long tights the next time he performs, but he went back to his classic look for AEW Rampage Grand Slam.
