Clint Howard Biography

Clint Howard is an American character actor who was born on April 20, 1959, in Burbank, California, the United States as Clinton Engle Howard. Clint is the second son born to American actors Rance and Jean Howard, and the younger brother of actor and director Ron Howard. He has over 200 acting credits–appearing in feature films including The Water boy and Apollo 13, and playing characters on television such as Mark Wedloe on Gentle Ben, Stanley on The Baileys of Balboa (1964–1965), Steve on The Cowboys (1974), as well as Creepy Rodney on My Name Is Earl (2006–2008).

Clint has appeared in many films directed by his brother, Ron, and had a small role in Walt Disney Pictures’ The Jungle Book. He also was lead singer and frontman of his band, The Kempsters, as well as recently has found success in his pursuit of making custom snowglobes.
