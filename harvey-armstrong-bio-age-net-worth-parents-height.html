Get To Know Harvey Armstrong! His Age, Net Worth, Parents

Made in Chelsea spinoff Made in Chelsea: Bali premiered on December 20, 2022, and right off the bat, returning star Harvey Armstrong became a reason for Yasmine and Issy’s heated argument!

So, intrigued by this charming persona? Keep reading this Harvey Armstrong Bio to learn all about him.

Harvey Armstrong On Made In Chelsea

Harvey Armstrong joined Made in Chelsea franchise Made in Chelsea: Buenos Aires back in 2019 and soon his love life gained major traction. At the time, the star had just broken things off with his first love of 4 years, Sophie Habboo, who already was a recurring star on the show.

Shocked to see Harvey on Made in Chelsea, Sophie recalled the time she had dropped Harvey at an airport in January 2019 while they were still together. Sadly, after Harvey went away to travel their “lives moved on”.

So, on the show, Harvey then found love again with Emily Blackwell. The two seemed like a good match and Emily was already so in love that she wanted to settle down and have his children. However, Harvey was more interested in partying. This made Emily feel disrespected and mistreated and soon Cracks in their relationship started to show  — which caused Emily to temporarily move out of the flat she shared with Harvey.

Unfortunately, to add fuel to the fire, Harvey went against Emily’s wishes and hosted an after-party even inviting female guests to their home. Naturally, Emily was upset and was forced to doubt Harvey after she received messages alleging that Harvey was close to another girl in a nightclub.

Thereafter, Emily spoke to OK! Magazine about being cheated on. “I have no hard feelings towards Harvey at all. I wish him nothing but the absolute best because we’re all human, we all make mistakes and I definitely think I was his mistake. He regrets it now, and it will be a good learning lesson for him,” she said.

In fact, she thanked god that he cheated on her because she feels so much happier with another person now.

Emily, as of 2022, is in a relationship with Jordan Alexander, who is the managing director at a recruitment consultancy. Their relationship even received the seal of approval from Emily’s best pal Miles Nazaire, who once revealed his romantic feelings for her!

On the other hand, Harvey was still looking for Emily’s forgiveness. In fact, he even vowed to win her back! Over an ep, breaking down in tears while discussing the incident where he cheated on a brunette beauty, Harvey told how Emily was his “best friend” and that he wants to prove to her that he has changed. Regretting, even more, he added, “I panicked and someone told her before I could get to her. The only thing I can do now is proving to her that I can change and be the man that I deserve.”

Is Harvey Armstrong Dating Anyone?

Harvey Armstrong remained single for a long time after splitting from fellow co-star Emily Blackwell. Even as of 2022, he was single.

As for his love triangle with Yasmine Zweegers and Issy Francis-Baum, Harvey confessed that he wasn’t attracted to Yasmine and even just wanted to keep “having fun” with Issy. Upon hearing this, Yasmine took the rejection well while Issy broke down.

Harvey Armstrong Job

Harvey Armstrong is the CEO and co-founder of Prime Time Lager, a new innovative craft beer brand. They are the world’s first caffeine-infused low-calorie lager that boasts 30% reduced calories and 63% reduced carbs when compared to other premium lagers and are also vegan and gluten-free. 

He founded the company back in June 2019.

Before that, he worked at PwC as an associate for almost 3 years. Also, he’s been a sales assistant at Gibraltar International Airport for a short while.

As for his education, Harvey attended ICAEW from 2013 to 2017, Newcastle University from 2013 to 2017, and The Chartered Institute for Securities & Investment (CISI) in 2016.

When Harvey’s not working he likes to hit the gym and play rugby. In fact, the star has been a fullback for Gibraltar Rugby and has toured all across Europe.
