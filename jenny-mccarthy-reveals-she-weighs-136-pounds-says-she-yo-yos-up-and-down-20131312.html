Jenny McCarthy Reveals Weight, Says She Yo-Yos About 18 Pounds

Jenny McCarthy isn't shy about sharing intimate details of her life with the public — for example, her weight. In a new interview with Radar Online, the View co-host revealed she currently weighs about 136 pounds. Click here to find out how much celebrities really weigh.

"I'm a big yo-yoer. I do go up and down. I go from, like, 120 to 138," she shared. "Right now I'm at 136, I think."

McCarthy, 41, admitted she lets herself indulge a little come November and December. "I take this time to thoroughly enjoy it during the holidays," she told Radar. "I'm kind of part of the collective thinking when it comes to just surrendering to the not-so-great foods."

That said, she has a few tricks to keep her ever-changing figure at least looking fit. "I drink a lot of water!" she revealed.
