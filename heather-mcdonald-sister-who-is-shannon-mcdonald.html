Dr. Shannon McDonald, is a Senior Medical Officer at First Nations Health Authority, Vancouver Island.

She works with the regional health authority, the Chief Medical Health Officer and MHOs, and health directors to ensure that high quality, coordinated and complementary health services are being offered by their organization.

McDonald reports on population and public health issues that require a collaborative and coordinated response.

She also leads FNHA participation in local, provincial, or national committees on public health, mental wellness, and substance misuse.

Prior to joining the FNHA, Dr. McDonald served for five years as Executive Director of the Aboriginal Health Directorate, BC Ministry of Health.
