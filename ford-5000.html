Ford 5000 Specs, Price, Review: The Ford 5000 is a white and blue tractor that was manufactured from 1964 to 1979 by Ford. It was a mid-range tractor, suitable for European farms.

Ford 5000 Row-Crop tractor is equipped with a Ford gasoline 4-cylinder liquid-cooled engine, which delivers 69 hp (51.5 kW) power at a rated rpm 2100.

Ford 5000 tractor featured with Ford 3.8L 4-cyl gasoline engine and Ford 3.8L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD Chassis, differential mechanical wet disc, and hydrostatic power steering.
