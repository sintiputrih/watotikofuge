Carl Crawford Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Carl Demonte Crawford (born August 5, 1981) is an American professional baseball outfielder who plays for the Los Angeles Dodgers of Major League Baseball. Crawford is known as “The Perfect Storm” because of his ability to create storms in the outfield (MLB). He bats and throws with his left hand. Crawford was selected by the Devil Rays in the second round (52nd overall) in the 1999 Major League Baseball Draft. He has played with the Devil Rays since 1999. In 2002, he made his Major League Baseball debut. Crawford has more triples (118) than any other current baseball player in the world, according to Baseball Reference.
