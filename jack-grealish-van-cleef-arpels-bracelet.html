Jack Grealishs mega 5k bracelet is owned by THIS totally unexpected royal

 We have to admit, we have a soft spot for Jack Grealish. The Manchester City midfielder has become something of a national treasure, thanks to his loveable personality and lust-worthy locks.

The footballer’s style has become synonymous with designer labels, such as Gucci and Louis Vuitton to name a few. On the accessories front, Jack rarely shies away from an opportunity for bling, regularly decking himself out in diamonds and jewels galore.

Yet, we happened to stumble across a striking similarity between the 27-year-old’s jewellery choices and those belonging to a member of the royal family. It seems the world of football and royalty share more than a palpable sense of patriotism.

Jack tapped into one of the most timeless jewellery trends when he purchased two bracelets from French heritage brand Van Cleef & Arpels. Coined the ‘Vintage Alhambra bracelet,’ the stunning jewel boasts five clover motifs that have become the brand’s signature symbol, an 18-karat gold structure and a border of golden beading.

The star owns the piece in two colour ways, one with carnelian red clover detailing, which retails at £4,350, and one with solid gold leaves. The latter costs a grand £5,300, racking up a near £10,000 in jewellery bills for the footballer. (Not much when you're earning approximately £300,000 per week, mind you.)

Queen Consort Camilla is also a fan of the decadent Van Cleef & Arpels design. The royal owns an impressive collection of jewels, yet her Van Cleef piece remains one of her favourites. Camilla’s version, which comes in blue and gold, is also sourced from the brand’s signature Magic Alhambra collection.

King Charles’ wife previously adorned the opulent piece to attend The Sandringham Flower Show 2022 alongside her husband. She appropriately paired the bracelet with a tropical blue dress that effortlessly complemented the beautiful colour scheme of her jewellery choice.
