Is Polo G Still Alive? Debunking the Rumors Surrounding the Rappers Health

Polo G is one of the most popular and successful rappers in the industry today. He has released three studio albums, Die a Legend (2019), The Goat (2020), and Hall of Fame (2021), which have all received critical acclaim and commercial success. He is also known for his hit singles such as “Pop Out”, “Rapstar”, and “Wishing for a Hero”.

However, Polo G’s fame and fortune have also come with some challenges and controversies. In 2019, he was hospitalized for a drug overdose at a party, which prompted him to quit taking ecstasy. He has also been arrested several times for various charges, including battery, resisting arrest, and carrying a concealed weapon.

These incidents have sparked rumors and speculations about Polo G’s health and well-being. Some fans have even wondered if he is still alive or if he has passed away. So, what is the truth behind these rumors? Is Polo G still alive or dead?

Polo G is alive and well

The answer is simple: Polo G is alive and well. He is not dead, nor is he in a coma or any other life-threatening condition. He is still active on social media, where he regularly posts updates and interacts with his fans. He is also working on releasing new music and expanding his career.

According to Leedaily, Polo G has been noticeably absent from social media lately because he has been hard at work on his new album, Hood Poet, which was due for release on September 15, 2023. However, he postponed the album’s release due to legal issues. He explained the situation on his Instagram story, saying:

I’m sorry to all my fans but due to unforeseen circumstances, I have to push back the release date of my album. I’m dealing with some legal stuff that I can’t talk about right now, but I promise you it will be worth the wait. I appreciate your patience and support. Hood Poet coming soon.

I’m good y’all. Don’t believe everything you hear. I’m focused on the positive and the future. God got me.
