Dominican professional basketball player Al Horford plays for NFL team, Philadelphia 76ers. He is the recipient of five-time NBA All-Star honors in 2010, 2011, 2015, 2016, and 2018.

Al Horford Bio & Family

The player was born as Alfred Joel Horford Reynoso 3 June 1986 in Puerto Plata, the Dominican Republic to parents, Tito Horford and Arelis Reynoso. His dad played three years in the NBA and several more overseas. Al went to Grand Ledge High School in Grand Ledge, Michigan, and was a star on its basketball team.

He then attended Florida College to play Basketball from 2004 until 2007. The athlete stands at the height of 6 feet 9 inches (2.06 m) tall and weighs around 111 kg.
