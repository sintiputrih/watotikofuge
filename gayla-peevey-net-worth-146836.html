Gayla Peevey Net Worth

Gayla Peevey is a former child star who was born on March 8, 1943 in Oklahoma City, OK. She is best known for her recording of the novelty song "I Want a Hippopotamus for Christmas," which she recorded when she was only ten years old. The song has become a holiday classic and has been covered by many artists over the years.
