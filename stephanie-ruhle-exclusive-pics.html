Exclusive Photos Of The MSNBC Anchor Hollywood Life

Stephanie Ruhle is an NBC News correspondent and anchor who has been with the network since 2016. She is currently the host of “MSNBC Live with Stephanie Ruhle” and the co-host of “Velshi & Ruhle” with ALi Velshi. Prior to joining MSNBC, Stephanie was a managing editor and anchor for Bloomberg Television, and an editor-at-large for Bloomberg News, where she also co-hosted the show “Bloomberg GO”. Before becoming a journalist, Stephanie was an investment banker. She actually met her husband, Andy Hubbard, on her first day in New York City, in 1998, on the trading floor. She worked at the hedge fund Credit Suisse, and was a vice president at Credit Suisse First Boston. Later, she joined Deutsche Bank as a managing director. Stephanie is also a columnist for Shape.com. She’s known for getting people to open up in interviews, including President Donald Trump, Kanye West, and Martha Stewart.
