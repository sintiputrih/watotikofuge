Biography

Hank Jones was born in Vicksburg, MS on July 31, 1918. The Dean of Jazz Pianists. He accompanied numerous stars, including Frank Sinatra and Ella Fitzgerald during her tour of England. He played the piano for Marilyn Monroe‘s infamous rendition of Happy Birthday at May 19, 1962, Madison Square Garden’s birthday party for John F. Kennedy. He started playing at local establishments by the time he was 13, and by his 20s, he had moved from Michigan to New York.
