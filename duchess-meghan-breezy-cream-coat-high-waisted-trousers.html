Duchess Meghan Wears a Breezy Cream Coat and High-Waisted Pants for Invictus Day 7

Duchess Meghan just stepped out in yet another perfect example of early fall dressing.

For day seven of the 2023 Invictus Games, the duchess attended the swimming medal ceremony in yet another polished monochromatic outfit courtesy of Cuyana, comprised of a silk trench coat with oversize lapels and high-waisted trousers, which were cinched with a matching belt and ended just above her ankles. She styled the ivory separates with a plain white tank and elevated the ensemble with tan pointed-toe pumps.

She accessorized the look sparingly, adding a pair of dainty gold earrings and her go-to Cartier Tank watch.

Jordan Pettitt - PA Images//Getty Images

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

Meanwhile, Prince Harry coordinated with his wife in his own light ensemble, pairing a white button-down with gray slacks, tan slip-on shoes, and several simple bracelets.

Later in the day, the duchess visited the kids’ area at the tournament, where she joined a children’s book reading.

Chris Jackson//Getty Images

Meghan has been on a streak of casually elegant monochromatic looks since touching down in Germany on Tuesday.

To attend cycling and volleyball events yesterday, the duchess paired a white romper from Zara, which featured a belted waist and gray-and-white buttons down the center, with a crisp single-breasted blazer in a matching hue. She completed the outfit with black Saint Laurent sandals, black Krewe sunglasses, and an array of gold jewelry including a gold zodiac pendant necklace from Brilliant Earth.

On Wednesday, she wore the ideal shorts suit for late summer, composed of a white blazer with black trim from J.Crew, a pair of cream pleated shorts from Staud, and beige slingback flats with a black cap-toe from Chanel. Her accessories included a black Givenchy belt and diamond stud earrings.
