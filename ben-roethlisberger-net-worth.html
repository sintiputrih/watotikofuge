Benjamin Todd Roethlisberger Sr., nicknamed “Big Ben,” is an American football quarterback for the National Football League’s Pittsburgh Steelers. He attended Miami University and was picked in the first round of the 2004 NFL Draft by the Steelers.

With two Super Bowl titles and 11 playoff appearances in his previous 17 years, he has decided to retire after the 2021 season.

“Big Ben” ranks seventh in NFL history with 63,721 yards per throw, sixth with 8,353 passes attempted, fifth with 5,386 pass completions, and ninth with 416 touchdown passes with one game remaining in Week 18.
