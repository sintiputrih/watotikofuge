Who are the voice actors for Hi-Fi Rush? Full voice cast

Recommended Videos

Hi-Fi Rush is a surprising, but welcome creation from well-known horror game developers, Tango Gameworks. Its mixture of colorful aesthetics and catchy, rhythm-based gameplay made it an instant fan-favorite as soon as it was announced. However, another aspect that made this title immediately stand out were the performances of its cast list. In order to give them the proper credit they deserve, we’ve listed down the entire Hi-Fi Rush voice cast below.

Hi-Fi Rush full voice cast

Robbie Daymond

Anime fans may already be familiar with Robbie Daymond, who plays the part of the energetic protagonist, Chai. Daymond has already held a variety of significant roles in various video games and animated shows, including Goro Akechi in Persona 5 and Megumi Fushiguro in My Hero Academia.

Erica Lindbeck

The wonderful Erica Lindbeck voices the equally amazing Peppermint in the game. Lindbeck is no slouch herself when it comes to her voice acting credits as she has appeared in multiple prominent titles, such as Marvel’s Midnight Suns, Persona 5, and Neon Genesis Evangelion, where she played Captain Marvel, Futaba Sakura, and Ritsuko Akagi respectively.

Gabe Kunda

Gabe Kunda, who takes the role of Macaron in Hi-Fi Rush, has plenty of experience when it comes to voicing video game characters. He is most known for playing the part of Newcastle in Apex Legends and KAY/O in Valorant. Much like Robbie Daymond, Kunda has similarly voiced a character in My Hero Academia named Ken Takagi, better known as Rock Lock.

Misty Lee

Misty Lee not only has a magical voice but she herself knows how to do magic as well. The multi-talented sleight of hand expert lends her brilliant skills to voice Vandelay Tech’s very own Head of Production, Rekka. Lee has also played a number of roles in other video game titles, which include Sigrun in God of War and the Ninth Sister in Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order.

Sunil Malhotra

CNMN (pronounced Cinnamon) is Macaron’s robotic psychoanalytical partner who is voiced by the brilliant Sunil Malhotra. He is most known for playing the part of Harbor, one of the operators in Valorant, and Kung Lao in Mortal Kombat 11.

Todd Haberkorn

Todd Haberkorn’s extensive list of acting credits shows just how talented an individual he is. In Hi-Fi Rush, he displays his voice acting ability by playing the part of Zanzo, the crazy Head of Research & Development at Vandelay Tech. Haberkorn can also be heard in many other popular video game titles including Destiny 2 and Genshin Impact, where he played The Drifter and Razor respectively.

David Fane

Although David Fane doesn’t have a ton of voice acting credits to his name yet, he sure doesn’t show it in his role as Roquefort in Hi-Fi Rush. One of the roles that Fane is known for includes a part in a TV show called Our Flag Means Death where he plays a pirate named Fang, one of Blackbeard’s pirates.

Roger Craig Smith

Another name that video game fans may be familiar with is Roger Craig Smith who plays the sinister Kale Vandelay in the game. Smith has played the role of some of the most iconic video game characters in history, including Ezio from the Assassin’s Creed series and Chris Redfield from the Resident Evil series. He has also been the voice of Sonic the Hedgehog since 2010.

Camilla Arfwedson – Mimosa

Camilla Arfwedson’s role as Mimosa in Hi-Fi Rush is her first ever voice acting gig since she started her career. Most of the prominent parts that she’s played include roles in theater productions and TV series, like The Merchant of Venice and Holby City, where she was cast as Portia and Zosia March respectively.

Sarah Elmaleh

Sarah Elmaleh, who voices Korsica in Hi-Fi Rush, is no stranger to playing important roles in video games. She was cast as the voice of the female player character in BioWare’s third-person shooter, Anthem. She is also known for voicing The Cube Queen in Fortnite Chapter 2.
