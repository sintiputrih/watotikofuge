God of War Niflheim Alloy, Aesirbane, Haze Weave Locations

Niflheim alloy, aesirbane and haze weave are crafting materials in God of War. They’re all used to buy and upgrade several kinds of armor unique to Niflheim, the realm of fog. These sets are among the best the game has to offer, which means you’ll probably want to collect varying quantities of the three materials, in order to make the most of the outfits. In this guide, we’re going to show you God of War haze weave, aesirbane & Niflheim alloy locations, to help you max out your gear.

Where to find Haze Weave, Aesirbane & Niflheim Alloy?

All three of them are used for pretty much the same – upgrading various armour sets, pommels and talismans with the power of the cursed mist. Consider them different rarities of the same items. The alloy is a “fragment of the misty metal used to craft Ivaldi’s armor”, while the weave is a “piece of cloth made indestructible by Dwarven craft”. The aesirbane is like the epic variant – the game calls it “Ivaldi’s ultimate creation, a concentration of mists made poisonous to Aesir gods”. It’s pretty potent stuff. The point is, you’ll probably need a whole bunch of these if you want to stay competitive in the endgame.

The good thing is, you can get them all in one place. They’re native to the realm of Niflheim, so you won’t have to look for them all over the place. Sadly, they’re found in golden chests in the maze, which means grinding the challenges there until your fingers are raw. Such is the life of a Greek god in Midgard. Once you’ve got them, you’ll be able to get equipment that will help immensely when dealing with the Valkyrie bosses, especially Queen Sigrun at the High Council.
