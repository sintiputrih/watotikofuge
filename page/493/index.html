published on February 18, 2024
Maui is known for its Hawaiian and Polynesian cuisine, and you’ll have ample opportunity to try some dishes during your Lahaina cruise. Pork and seafood play a big role in the main course. For a quick bite, try a Kalua pork taco or a fish taco made with that day’s fresh catch. Another way to taste the local cuisine is by finding a place that serves a traditional Hawaiian lunch, which typically consists of a main entree of fish or meat (can be chicken, pork, or beef), a couple scoops of rice, and macaroni salad.
