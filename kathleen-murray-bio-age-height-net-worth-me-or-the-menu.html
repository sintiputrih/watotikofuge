Who Is Kathleen Murray? Bio, Age, Height, Net Worth

Kathleen Murray has a passion for cooking has taken her to many places. Some of the most highlighted moments are her competing on Gordon Ramsay’s show Masterchef and now in Food Network’s who Me or The Menu. But unlike in Masterchef, the five-episode series is a professional as well as a personal challenge for her and her partner Nate Albert.

The other casts joining her on the show are Jessica and James, Nicole and Alan, and Jeanette and Randi.

Kathleen Murray On Me Or The Menu

Nate Albert, the fiance of Kathleen Murray, has no interest in the food business. But he got her a food truck (more on that later), and they became co-owners of Saucy Kat Food Truck, which she hopes to turn into a restaurant. According to the press release of Me or The Menu, things were smooth until the food truck event.

The press release further stated that Nate made a mistake. It causes them to lose hundreds of dollars. Moreover, Nate also has an attitude problem that is affecting their relationship. This lack of motivation in Kathleen believes that he doesn’t commit to her. Now, Nate has to do everything in his power to gain Kathleen’s trust back and show that he is as much a business partner as he is a romantic partner.

Kathleen talked about her appearance on the show with Daily Herald. She explained, “Probably the most important thing about this show is the in-depth look at the impact of our personal relationship while trying to run a business together”. She and Nate did an online interview and they landed the part on the show.

About filming the show compared to Masterchef, she added, “Having cameras on me from the moment I wake until the moment I go to bed, having GoPro (cameras) in the car and being mic-ed up all day long — it really delved into the behind-the-scenes of life and what it’s like to run a business”.

Are Nate Albert And Kathleen Murray Still Together?

Nate Albert and Kathleen Murray have such a strong relationship that challenges on a TV show don’t perturb their relationship. So, yes they are still together. In addition, their relationship is confirmed by their relationship status on Facebook which reads, “In a relationship”.
