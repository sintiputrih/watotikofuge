Zoey Stark Confirms Torn ACL And Meniscus, Vows To Come Back Better Than Ever

WWE NXT 2.0 star has confirmed that she’s recovering from a torn ACL/Meniscus.

In a tweet on November 9, the former NXT Women’s Tag Team Champion wrote that she suffered the injury when Toxic Attraction attacked her, but she stated that she’s already rehabbing in the aftermath of her surgery. Stark then vowed to return “better & stronger.”

Sadly, I tore my ACL/Meniscus due to Toxic's attack. Here's the bad news for the "champs". Surgery went great and I'm already rehabbing. I'll be back BETTER & STRONGER with three targets in mind! Until then @shirai_io @KacyCatanzaro @wwekayden kick their ass tonight!@WWENXT

— Zoey Stark (@ZoeyStarkWWE) November 9, 2021

The attack Stark referenced occurred on the November 2 episode of WWE 2.0, and it was meant to write her off TV due to her legitimate injury. It’s currently unclear how long Stark will be sidelined with the injury.

WrestleZone will provide more details as they become available.
