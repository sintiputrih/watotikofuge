Nate McMillan is a former NBA guard and head coach. He was born on August 3, 1964 in Raleigh, NC and played for the Seattle SuperSonics from 1986 to 1998. He then served as head coach of the SuperSonics from 2000 to 2005 and the Portland Trail Blazers from 2005 to 2012.
