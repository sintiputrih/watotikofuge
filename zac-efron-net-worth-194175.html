Zac Efron Net Worth

Zac Efron is an actor and singer who was born on October 18, 1987 in San Luis Obispo, CA. He is best known for his role as Troy Bolton in High School Musical, for which he won a Bravo Otto Award in 2010 for Best Male TV Star. He has also starred in other films such as Hairspray, Baywatch, and Neighbors.

About

Actor and singer who rose to fame for playing Troy Bolton in High School Musical and won a Bravo Otto Award in 2010 for Best Male TV Star. He has also played roles in the movies Hairspray, Baywatch and Neighbors. 

Before Fame

He was known as the class clown in elementary school, but became upset if he got less than an A on his exams.

Trivia

He voiced the lead character Ted in the 2012 animated film The Lorax. 

Family Life

He dated Actress and co-star Vanessa Hudgens for three years, but the relationship ended in December 2010. He then went on to date Sami Miro from 2014 until 2016. His father David worked as an Engineer for a power plant, where his mother Starla worked as a secretary. He has a younger brother named Dylan. 

Associated With

He starred alongside Matthew Perry in 17 Again.
