Jason Gaskell Career

Jason Gaskell started his career in the world of finance. He founded Gaskell Stringing Service in 2002. He ran the company till 2008.

From 2005 to 2008, Jason also landed an internship at Scannell Wealth Management Group, LLC. In 2009, Jason worked at Lane Gorman Trubitt, L.L.P. as a staff auditor. His job as an auditor lasted till 2010. In January 2010, MMD Holdings hired him as controller. He continued working for the company till 2012.

Then, he joined Essilor as a Senior Financial Analyst till 2014.

From March 2014 to February 2020, Jason worked at Ambit Energy as Senior Trading & Financial Risk Analyst. He was entrusted as the primary analyst and the sole power trader responsible for trading $750+ million a year. He managed the overall position and exposure of Ambit’s power division along with analyzing any and all other aspects that fall under it.

Jason joined Ambit with no prior industry knowledge but had the drive, mental capacity, and analytical know-how to make material impacts within months of joining the team. He also created a curve model for all power markets, price automation tools, ICE RTD models for both Power & Gas markets, brought PJM & MISO ARR/CRR processes in-house, hedge proposal models, researched & analyzed Wind Power Purchase Agreements (PPA), commodity forecasting models, etc.

For the next six months after leaving Ambit Energy, he worked for Energy Monger as Director of Finance.

As of 2022, Jason is the owner of Sextant Sailing and Sextant Consulting.

Jason graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Finance and Accounting from Purdue University in 2008. He secured MBA majoring in Economics from Texas A&M University-Commerce in 2020.

In March 2020, Jason worked earned Yachtmaster Certification from Royal Yachting Association.
