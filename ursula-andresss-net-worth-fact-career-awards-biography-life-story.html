Ursula Andress is a Swiss-American actress and former model. She is best known for her breakthrough role as Bond girl Honey Ryder in the first James Bond film, Dr. No. She is also known as a sex symbol. She won the Golden Globe Award on the category of “New Star of the Year” for her appearance in the film “Dr. No” in 1964. She was also chosen by Empire magazine as one of the “100 Sexiest Stars in film history” in 1995.

Ursula Andress became a star in the 1960s to 1990s. She has starred in several films including Fun in Acapulco, She, The 10th Victim, Up to His Ears, The Blue Max, Casino Royale, The Sensuous Nurse, Once Before I Die, The Mountain of the Cannibal God, The Fifth Musketeer and Clash of the Titans.

Where was Ursula Andress born?

Ursula Andress was born in Ostermundigen, Berne, Switzerland on 19 March 1936. She was born to father, Rolf Andress who was a German diplomat and mother, Anna Andress who was a landscape gardener. She has a brother, Heinz Andress, and four sisters, Erika Andress, Charlotte Andress, Gisela Andress, and Katey Andress.

Ursula holds a Swiss nationality and belongs to a White ethnic background. Her zodiac sign is Pisces.

Regarding her education, she attended school in Bern till the age of sixteen. Later she moved to Paris and studied art for a year. Then, she went to Rome, Italy where she did several jobs which include being a nanny. She can speak several languages including French, German, and Italian.
