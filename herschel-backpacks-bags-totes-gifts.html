When it comes to taking over the streetwear landscape, no other carryall brand has made quite the impression like Herschel Supply Co. From casual athleisure style to our everyday wardrobes, this trendy brand can be seen on the backs and arms of many fashionistas on a daily basis.

The brainchild of brothers Jamie and Lyndon Cormack, the duo has always been devoted to creating timeless designs with a fine regard for detail. Once the Vancouver-based brand debuted in 2009, we immediately saw a shift. Starting out with their classic collection, each piece was created to include modern functionality and unique detailing while keeping in mind the consumer’s daily routine.

The brand quickly upped the ante with three collections: The Studio Collection that highlights unique and unconventional styles, the Bad Hills Workshop that combines old and new styles for a modern touch and the Kids Collection full of lively patterns and a functional style to allow our little ones to make an adorably stylish statement.

What we love most about this brand is that there is a construction to suit every aspect of our lives. Whether we’re heading to the gym, going out of town for the weekend or heading to school and work, there is a design that will carry our essentials with ease. From backpacks, totes, duffel bags and more, the possibilities are truly endless for a classic, yet refined appeal.

Ready to give your bag vault a trendy update? Check out our favorite picks that stand out from the pack. It’s time to Shop With Us!

Herschel Supply Co. Novel Duffel Mid-Volume

If you’re like Us, your go-to travel bag may be on its last legs. As we’re always on the hunt for a stylish, yet durable carryall that can store our essentials with ease, the Herschel Supply Co. Novel Duffel Mid-Volume falls right in line.

Made to keep Us organized while we’re on the go, this bag features an internal storage sleeve to keep our small items safe while traveling. Functional in nature, there is a removable padded strap for slinging over the shoulder and top handles for an arm and body carry.

We also love its side-access shoe compartment. There is nothing worse than packing your shoes in the same section as your clothes and with this detail, we can separate the two to keep our ensembles clean. Equipped with a two-way waterproof zipper, we can also protect our belongings from the elements seamlessly. A great staple for our weekend adventures and beyond, this design will come right in handy.

Shoppers who purchased this carryall love its sturdy, top-quality construction, while others like that the shoe compartment keeps clothes and footwear separate for the ultimate level of organization. Dubbed as the perfect go-to travel duffel, this bag is an amazing buy.

Available in a wide range of colors including neutral tones, prints, patterns and muted shades, there is an option to suit every style taste.

Herschel Supply Co Nova Backpack

Every fashionista should have a stylish backpack on standby. A fabulous design that will come in major clutch on days when we have to carry a little extra, this style will make toting around our essentials a breeze.

We are excited to add the Herschel Supply Co. Nova Backpack Mid-Volume to our bag vault. Designed for a day full of adventure, this bag features dual top carry handles, external water bottle pockets and shoulder straps to keep Us ahead of the game.

Our favorite detail? The 13-inch laptop sleeve. For Us, our workday never stops and it’s important to carry our devices around safely. We also dig its light back padding. With many styles on the market, we tend to feel the contents in our bag on our back, but this offering tops our list thanks to its layer of comfort. The front storage pocket is also a major plus for storing our wallet and keys for easy access.

The minimalist flair and roomy construction make this bag a winner with Herschel shoppers. Many reviewers love its comfortable feel, while others like that its perfect for day-to-day activities and errands.
