Sara Foster Pregnant, Expecting Second Child With Tommy Haas

Double the joy! Sara Foster is pregnant and expecting her second child with Tommy Haas, Us Weekly can confirm. The Barely Famous star, 34, is already a proud mom to 4-year-old daughter Valentina.

The 90210 alum recently gave all moms and dads taking paternity and maternity leave a shout-out, sharing a link to Wired’s article about how Netflix allows parents to take a yearlong leave. “It’s good to work for #Netflix,” she tweeted to her followers without addressing her own pregnancy.

 

The uncensored reality star has been vocal about the not-so-glamorous side of being a mom. During a chat with BuzzFeed in March, she admitted her Google searches aren’t as hot as her sister Erin’s. “The last thing [I googled] is so unsexy and your readers will not give a s—t about this but it was ear infections because my daughter has an ear infection.”
