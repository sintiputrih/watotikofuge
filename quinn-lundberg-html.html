Quinn Lundberg - Trivia, Family, Bio

Charity worker known as the wife of actor Zach Galifianakis. In August 2012, they married at the UBC Farm in Vancouver. She co-founded Growing Voices charity. She volunteered with UNICEF as part of the web production unit.

Before Fame

She graduated with a certificate in international conflict resolution from CUNY Baccalaureate Program in New York. She also has a degree in Public Policy. 

Trivia

She has attended various events with her husband, including the 2010 premiere of the film Dinner for Schmucks in New York and the 2015 Film Independent Spirit Awards at Santa Monica. She is a fan of ballet.

Family Life

She and her husband have two sons.

Associated With

Her husband is a star of The Hangover franchise starring Bradley Cooper and Ed Helms.
