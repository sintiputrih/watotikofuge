Dot-Marie Jones Marries: Glee Star Weds Longtime Girlfriend Bridgett C

Dot-Marie Jones is happily married! The Glee star and longtime girlfriend Bridgett Casteen got married Saturday, Dec. 21, her rep confirms to Us Weekly.

Jones, 49, and Casteen exchanged vows Saturday night at their LA home before a group of close friends. The actress, who plays Coach Shannon Beiste on the hit Fox series, got engaged to Casteen back in October at Disneyland, near Sleeping Beauty's Castle. "Thank you @dotmariejones for making me the happiest woman in the world," Casteen shared via Twitter along with a picture of the two.
