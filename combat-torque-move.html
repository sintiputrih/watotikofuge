From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Combat Torque (Japanese: ファイトアクセル Fight Accel) is a damage-dealing Fighting-type move introduced in Generation IX. It is the signature move of the Caph Starmobile.

Effect

Combat Torque inflicts damage and has a 30% chance of paralyzing the target.

Encore, Instruct, and Mimic fail if the target's last used move was Combat Torque. Copycat fails if the last used move was Combat Torque.
