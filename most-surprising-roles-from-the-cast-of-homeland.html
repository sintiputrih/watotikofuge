Most surprising roles from the cast of Homeland

    is finally back on our screens for its sixth season, and after six years of watching the show it almost seems like we know the characters and the actors playing them. But what sort of roles did our favourite cast take on at the beginning of their careers? Find out more about past roles from the stars of the show, some of them might surprise you!

    Mandy Patinkin

    Mandy is known on for playing Saul - a calming, fatherly type who mentors Carrie, but what fans may not know is that the actor once played the iconic character Inigo Montoyo in the eighties cult classic Yep, good old Saul was once that sword-fighting soft spoken bandit who said the famous line: "My name is Inigo Montoyo, you killed my father, prepare to die!"

    Claire Danes

    Claire's character Carrie on the show is a highly intelligent yet troubled woman who struggles with her bipolar disorder, but way back in 1994 the role that made Claire's name was Angela Chase in In the show, a red-haired Angela attempts to discover her own identity while dealing with the trials and tribulations of teenage life.

    Morena Baccarin

    Before she played Nick Brody's conflicted and bemused wife Jessica, Morena played Inara Serra in the cult Sci-Fi TV show, In the show Morena played a 'companion' (part geisha, part escort) while joining in on adventures on the Serenity spaceship.
