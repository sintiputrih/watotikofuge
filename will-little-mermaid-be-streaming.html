When Will The Little Mermaid Be Streaming on Disney Plus?

‘The Little Mermaid’ Will Be Streaming on Disney Plus — Details

By Melissa Willets

Aug. 7 2023, Updated 12:30 p.m. ET

Raise your fin if you're pumped to see The Little Mermaid live action adaptation. Us too! Of course, if you missed the movie in theaters, you're probably wondering when you'll be able to stream it on Disney Plus. Luckily, we have an answer for you!

Ahead, we'll give you the inside scoop on how to watch The Little Mermaid, which stars Halle Bailey at Ariel, Javier Bardem as King Triton, Jonah Hauer-King as Eric, Melissa McCarthy as Ursula, and Awkwafina as Scuttle.

When will 'The Little Mermaid' live action be streaming?

The long wait to see The Little Mermaid as a live-action reimagining is finally over! The film was released in theaters Friday, May 26, 2023.

Disney Plus subscribers weren't able to stream the new movie at first, which didn't sit well with all fans.

We have some good news for folks who'd rather enjoy the movie in the comfort of their own homes. The Little Mermaid will eventually be streaming on Disney Plus. So, how long will excited fans have to wait?

'The Little Mermaid' will stream on Disney Plus starting on September 6.

Typically, Disney movies have come to Disney Plus between 45 and 90 days after their initial theatrical release, so we were expecting to be able to enjoy the new mermaid flick sometime between early July and late August.

However, for whatever reason, Disney is waiting a bit longer with The Little Mermaid — it won't be available to stream until Sept. 6.

Even though it's a bit longer of a wait, we can't wait to revisit the film from the comfort of our own homes! We're so excited to make Ariel a permanent part of our world.
