Biography

J-Sol was born in Essex, England on December 13, 1992. Pop music singer/songwriter whose debut single “Alien” received radio support from BBC Radio 1Xtra, Choice FM, and Rinse FM. He also had the opportunity to open for Boyz II Men at London’s O2 Arena. He has covered songs made famous by Rita Ora and Sam Smith. In 2012, he was the recipient of the BEFFTA Award for Outstanding Young Achiever.
