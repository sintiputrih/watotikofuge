New Mufin Player Sorts Music By Sound

published on May 12, 2024
HOT OUT OF GERMANY'S OVEN (Hypebot) – Now in public beta, the new Muffin player sorts music libraries by sound and recommends songs using audio fingerprinting technology originated at the Fraunhofer Institute, the inventor of the MP3. Mufin creates a fingerprint for each song based on 40 characteristics including tempo, instruments, sound density, and rhythm.
