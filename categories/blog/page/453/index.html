How X Came To Mean Kiss

During the Middle Ages, an X as a signature often marked letters as well as oaths to show loyalty and love, with the sender kissing the mark to assure their word. The habit became a precursor to the acronym SWAK ("sealed with a kiss"), something World War I soldiers inscribed on their envelopes home.
