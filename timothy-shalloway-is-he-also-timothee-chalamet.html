Timothy Shalloway: Is He also Timothe Chalamt?

Timothy Shalloway, most popularly known as Timothée Chalamét, is a famous Hollywood actor known for his outstanding roles. Here’s everything we know about him. 

You may know Timothy Shalloway as “Timothée Chalamét” instead. Either way, you’re correct, as both names refer to the same actor. 

Who Is Timothy Shalloway?

Timothy Shalloway is a famous American actor born in New York City on December 27, 1995, to parents Nicole Flenzwe and Marc Chalamét. His mother is a real estate broker, actress, and dancer, while his father is a United Nations Children’s Fund (UNICEF) editor-in-chief and former New York correspondent for Le Parisien. 

He has an elder sister, Pauline, an actress, with whom he spent most of his childhood. They are both bilingual and hold dual citizenship in the United States and France due to his father’s French heritage. 

Timothy attended PS 87 William T. Sherman School and later joined MS 54 Booker T. Washington Middle School. Not long after, he went to Fiorello LaGuardia High School of Music and Art and Performing Arts, citing Heath Ledger’s performance as the joker as the source of inspiration to pursue acting as a career.

At 17, Timothy went to Columbia University but was transferred to New York University’s Gallatin School of Individualized Study. He graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in acting after four years.

As a child, he appeared in several commercials and acted in two horror short films, “Sweet Tooth” and “Clow. Timothy then debuted on an episode of “Law & Order” (2009), playing a murder victim. The young actor has been in several movies and TV shows, making him a fan favorite with his good looks and unique name.

Timothy Shalloway’s Nominations and Awards

His superb acting has also earned him some industry nominations for prominent awards. For his critically acclaimed role in “Call Me by Your Name,” he was nominated for Best Actor, an Academy Award, a BAFTA Award, a Golden Globe Award, a Screen Actors Guild Award, and a Critics’ Choice Movie Award. 

He also gained a lot of recognition for his role in “Lady Bird,” receiving nominations for the Critics’ Choice Movie Award for Best Acting Ensemble and the Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Cast in a Motion Picture along with the rest of the cast. Timothy got more recognition and nominations in the following years, such as the Critic’s Choice Award and a Screen Actors Guild Award.
