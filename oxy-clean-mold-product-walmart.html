SHOPPERS are realizing a common household product helps fight mold in the bathroom.

The $5 product is sold at stores, like Walmart, and customers say it has left their showers "pristine."

Oxi-Clean plus Bleach is a no-drop foam that helps to fight mold and mildew stains in the bathroom.

It's sold at Walmart for $4.98.

The product is described as a foaming cleaner that "clings to vertical surfaces, crevices, and corners."

It says its 100% tough bleach-powered foam stays "where you spray for easy stain removal without needing to scrub hard."

The instructions say you just need to spray. Dirt and grime can be wiped away with a wet sponge or damp cleaning cloth.

RAVE REVIEWS

The product has received 4.6 stars out of five stars on Walmart's site.

Shoppers who have used it said it's "convenient and a time saver."

Others said: "This stuff is amazing!"

GENTLE TOUCH

For those who prefer to stay away from chemicals to treat mold, there are home remedies that people are turning to.

Cinnamon is said to help because it is naturally antifungal and antibacterial.

You can use cinnamon oil for mold by mixing a few drops of the oil with water in a spray bottle.

The acidity in lemons is said to help combat mold.

It acts like a natural antiseptic, stain remover, bleach, and cleaning product.

People are talking about another product, tea tree oil.

Many may know it as a shampoo or body wash but there are other uses.

To use it as a mold-killer, it's suggested to mix one teaspoon of the tea tree with one cup of water in a spray bottle.

You can buy the essential oil on Amazon for about $8.49.
