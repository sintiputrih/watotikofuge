Mike Tagliere- Professional Career

Mike began his career as a sports correspondent and NFL writer in 2011 when he launched his own website. He gained a great deal of knowledge in the sector and then began working for Rotoworld and RotoGrinders. He began his career with PFF Fantasy in 2015 as a podcast host. Mike, along with Kyle Yates and Dan Harris, hosted podcasts on a regular basis. He spent two years there before moving on to FantasyPros as an NFL writer in 2017. Mike also began hosting the FantasyPros podcast, which quickly grew in popularity. He quickly became a household name in the NFL and transformed the entire landscape of NFL analysis. Mike had been actively writing about and analysing the NFL for the site, as well as appearing as a guest on various sports and media outlets and websites.
