Minnie Driver aka Amelia Fiona Jessica “Minnie” Driver is a British-American actress, singer and songwriter. As of 2022, Minnie Driver’s net worth is $16 million. She is best known for her work in cult classics like Grosse Pointe Blank and Gus Van Sant’s Good Will Hunting.

She was also nominated for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress and a Screen Actors Guild Award for her acting in the Matt Damon and Robin Williams starrer Good Will Hunting. Driver also earned nominations for an Emmy and a Golden Globe award for the American television drama, The Riches.

Minnie Driver is also known as a reputable singer, who started her career by singing in her solo albums as well as in numerous animated series and movies like the SuperMansion and Tarzan and in video games like Jurassic Park: Trespasser.
