Vicente Fernandez Net Worth

Vicente Fernandez is a renowned Mexican singer, actor, and movie producer who has been in the entertainment industry for over fifty years. He was born on February 17, 1940 in Guadalajara, Mexico and has achieved great success, winning seven Latin Grammy Awards. His career has been long and successful, and he is still active in the industry today.
