Did Ryan Gosling date Rachel McAdams?

Born in Canada on November 12th, 1980, Ryan Thomas Gosling has become a popular actor. Well-known in the independent cinema industry, he has also worked on blockbuster films across all genres, helping them earn over $1.9 billion at the international box office.

Did Ryan Gosling date Rachel McAdams?

It was an interesting twist of fate that Gosling and McAdams pursued a romantic relationship outside of their on-screen pairing.

After filming wrapped, the two actors continued to date on and off for the following two years before finally breaking up in 2007.

When they accepted the prize for Best Kiss at the 2005 MTV Movie Awards, they reenacted the scene live on stage, and the audience went wild at the palpable chemistry between the two of them.

Gosling and McAdams failed to click as a couple, unlike their characters in The Notebook. Gosling remarked, “The only thing I remember is we both went down swingin’ and we called it a draw,” when questioned about the split in a 2007 interview with GQ.
