America Ferrera Honors Barbie in Metallic Pink Gown at 2024 Oscars

This Barbie is an Oscar nominee. America Ferrera channeled her fierce Barbie character, Gloria, on the Oscars red carpet tonight.

She paired the shimmery bodycon dress with silver platform heels by Stuart Weitzman, a chunky diamond Pomellato necklace with red rubellites, and a matching ring. As for glam, she opted for minimal rosy makeup and a side-parted bob.
