How much does it cost to send a card to Beckett to get graded?

When it comes to collecting and selling collectible cards, especially in the world of sports and entertainment, grading services play a significant role in determining their value and authenticity. Beckett Grading Services (BGS) is one such renowned service that collectors often turn to. But what does it cost to send a card to Beckett for grading? In this article, we will explore the cost structure and steps involved in sending your cards to Beckett for grading.

Understanding Beckett Grading Services

Beckett Grading Services is a trusted and widely recognized company that provides professional card grading services to collectors and enthusiasts. With their expertise and strict grading standards, Beckett assigns a grade to each card, indicating its condition and overall quality. This grading process helps collectors and sellers determine the value of their cards in the market.

Cost of Sending Cards to Beckett

The cost of sending a card to Beckett for grading depends on several factors, including the type and value of the card, the desired turnaround time, and any additional services requested. Beckett offers different levels of services, each with its own pricing structure. Let's take a closer look at these levels and their associated costs:

Standard Service

The Standard Service is the most basic level offered by Beckett. It is ideal for collectors who are not in a rush to get their cards graded. The cost per card for the Standard Service is typically around $20 to $35, depending on the declared value of the card. This service usually takes around 30 business days for the grading process to be completed.

Economy Service

The Economy Service is a more affordable option for collectors looking for a budget-friendly grading solution. With this level of service, the cost per card is generally lower than the Standard Service. However, the turnaround time is longer, ranging from 60 to 90 business days. The specific cost for the Economy Service can vary, but it is typically around $15 to $30 per card.

Express Service

For collectors who wish to expedite the grading process, Beckett offers the Express Service. This level of service ensures a quicker turnaround time, usually within 20 business days. However, the cost per card is higher compared to the Standard and Economy Services. The pricing for the Express Service can range from $35 to $50 per card, depending on the declared value.

Super Express Service

If time is of the essence and you need your cards graded urgently, Beckett's Super Express Service is the way to go. This premium service guarantees an even faster turnaround time, typically within 10 business days. However, the cost per card is significantly higher compared to the other service levels. The price for the Super Express Service can range from $75 to $100 per card.

Important Considerations

When sending your cards to Beckett for grading, there are a few important considerations to keep in mind:

  • Insurance: It is crucial to insure your cards when shipping them to Beckett. This ensures that your valuable collectibles are protected in case of any unforeseen circumstances during transit.

  • Submission Form: To initiate the grading process, you will need to obtain a submission form from Beckett. This form can be obtained online or through their magazines. Fill out all the required information accurately to facilitate a smooth grading experience.

  • Minimum Grade: Beckett also offers a service to re-grade cards from other grading services. If you submit a card for re-grading, you can specify a minimum grade for each card. The review fee is the same as the grading fee. If the minimum grade is reached, the card will be removed from the original holder and placed in a Beckett case.

    • Sending a card to Beckett for grading can be an excellent investment for collectors and sellers alike. Understanding the cost structure and the different service levels offered by Beckett is essential in determining the right option for your needs. Whether you opt for the Standard, Economy, Express, or Super Express Service, Beckett Grading Services ensures that your collectible cards are professionally graded, adding value to your collection and providing peace of mind.
