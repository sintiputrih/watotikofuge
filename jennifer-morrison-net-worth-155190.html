About

Actress best known for her roles on the television shows House M.D., How I Met Your Mother, and Once Upon a Time. She also played Winona Kirk in the 2009 film Star Trek. 

Before Fame

She was a member of the marching band, cheerleading squad and school choir while in high school.

Family Life
