Kristina Kelly On Vanderpump Rules

Bravo’s Vanderpump Rules season 10 premiered on February 8, 2023, and long-time fans of the show rejoiced as they saw a familiar face, Kristina Kelly, hanging out with the group.

For those who’ve only recently started watching the show, Kristina has always been a fiercely loyal friend, especially to longtime bestie Katie Maloney. She was often seen jumping to Katie’s defense in the group —  serving as both sounding board and soldier.

However, this was back in season 7. Meaning, a lot has changed and now, she’s no longer willing to hold grudges on behalf of her friends. But will this be enough to solve problems? Guess, we’ll just have to wait and watch.

As per a press release for the new season, Kristina was on hand to support Katie through her split from Tom Schwartz. “After 12 years together, Tom and Katie Maloney have gone their separate ways with hopes for an amicable split as long as they both agree to obey one pact – no relationships within the friend group. When Tom hooks up with someone in the circle, their well-intentioned hope for friendship becomes nearly impossible to maintain. Katie begins to date again, while leaning on her friends Lala and Kristina Kelly for support,” the wrote.

This season, Kristina was also joined by James’ girlfriend Ally Lewber and SURver Charli Burnett. So, a lot went down in the new season including rumors, shocking confessions, ups, and downs. No wonder, the press release remarked the Vanderpump Rules crews were personal and professional lives wilder than ever before.
